lezzet · the taste list

a day in mouthfuls.

One Istanbul food tour in three meals. Plan a Saturday around it. First-timer-friendly, signature-dish forward, no airport-mall trash.

kahvaltı breakfast · 8–11am
01 · breakfast

van kahvaltı evi.

Cihangir · Defterdar Yokuşu 52/A

The breakfast spread that broke the internet, eight little plates of cheese, honeycomb, jam, eggs, and warm bread. Order kaymak (clotted cream) on the side. You will not eat lunch.

$$ must-try: kaymak

öğle midday · 1–4pm
02 · midday snack

karaköy güllüoğlu.

Karaköy · Rıhtım Cd. 3-5

The baklava bench-mark since 1820. Order pistachio at the counter, sit at the marble bench inside, watch the ferries. Walk it off across the Galata Bridge with a paper bag of lokum from the corner.

$ must-try: pistachio baklava + rose lokum

akşam dinner · 7pm onwards
03 · dinner + dessert

çiya sofrası.

Kadıköy · Güneşli Bahçe Sk. 43

Cross to the Asian side, eat regional Anatolian dishes you can't get anywhere else, then walk five minutes to a viral San Sebastián cheesecake spot. End the night with künefe by the ferry.

$$ must-try: künefe + basque cheesecake
